Rafting Trips in SE Idaho

Island Park to Ashton

Embark on a thrilling rafting adventure from Island Park to Ashton. With a distance of 13 miles, this trip offers a perfect blend of excitement and scenic beauty. Take on challenging rapids and enjoy the picturesque surroundings.

Heise to Idaho Falls

Experience the rush of rafting from Heise to Idaho Falls. Covering a distance of 10 miles, this trip provides a great opportunity for adventure enthusiasts. Navigate through thrilling rapids while enjoying the stunning natural landscapes.

Ashton to St. Anthony

Join us for an exhilarating journey from Ashton to St. Anthony. This 7-mile trip offers an adrenaline-pumping experience with exciting rapids. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the surroundings as you navigate through the river's twists and turns.
